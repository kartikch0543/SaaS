# SEO Strategy

## Positioning

StudyForge AI targets non-branded, education-focused long-tail keywords that align with student search behavior around viva questions, short notes, interview preparation, and beginner roadmaps.

## Keyword Principles

- Prioritize long-tail informational and low-to-medium competition queries
- Avoid trademarked product names or branded competition terms
- Balance TOFU guides, MOFU comparison-style study content, and BOFU feature-oriented landing pages

## Example Target Keywords

- dbms viva questions for beginners
- sql joins explained with examples
- operating system short notes for exam
- frontend roadmap for beginners
- how to prepare for technical viva
- oops interview questions for students
- computer networks important topics

## On-Page SEO

- Title tags and descriptions per route
- Canonical URLs via `SeoHead`
- FAQ schema, article schema, and breadcrumb schema for article pages
- ranking pages integrated into the product homepage and footer
- related article blocks for internal linking
- Sitemap and robots in `frontend/public`
